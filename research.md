### The Breit interaction

I completed my Honours thesis under the supervision of Dr. Benjamin Roberts in 2024/2025 on the Breit interaction in atomic physics. The Breit interaction represents the leading order relativistic correction to the typical electron-electron (Coulomb) interaction, and in my project I included it into all-orders perturbative methods in atomic physics. In my thesis I also included frequency-dependent corrections to the Breit interaction into the Hartree-Fock procedure, which hadn't been done before. ### Edge modes in vortex matter of superfluids

Over the summer of 2023/2024, I completed a summer research project under the supervision of Dr. Matthew Reeves and Professor Matthew Davis in Bose-Einstein condensation. The aim of my project was to try to numerically reproduce the solitonic modes on the edges of bulk superfluid vortex matter [predicted by Bogatskiy and Wiegmann](https://doi.org/10.1103/PhysRevLett.122.214505). While I never managed to convincingly find them through numerical simulations, I learnt a lot of interesting physics along the way and made a [presentation](./2024_summer_research_presentation.pdf) of what I had achieved along the way!
